About

The Chronicles of Narnia, written by famous author, C.S. Lewis, are stories about a mystical kingdom called Narnia, where animals coexists with people, witches fight battle, and dragons and horses ride overhead. It's a world where hunting teams ride seahorses and silvers can heal individuals. To readers looking for more than a fairy tale about an imagined and a magical realm, The Chronicles of Narnia may also be seen as a Christian allegory, with Aslan, the great talking lion, representing Christ.
