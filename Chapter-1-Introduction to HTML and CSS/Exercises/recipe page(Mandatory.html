Methods

  • Preheat the oven
  • Coat an 8 inch square baking pan with cooking oil spray. Line with parchment paper of (baking paper) and place it aside.
  • In a larger bowl, combine the hot melted butter, oil, and sugar. Wisk well for approximately a minute. Beat in the eggs and vanilla until the color lightens about another minute.
  • Sift in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gently incorporate the dry ingredients into the wet components until just blended (do not overbeat as this may damage the texture of the brownies).
  • Pour batter into the prepared pan and level the top evenly. (Optional: Top with chocolate pieces or chips.)
  • Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the centre of the brownies in the pan no longer jiggles and is just set to the touch (the brownies will keep baking in the hot pan out of the oven).
  • Remove and allow to cool to room temperature before slicing into 16 brownies.
